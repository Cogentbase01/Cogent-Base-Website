02  The bottleneck

The questionnaire is where the listing timetable goes to wait.

A sponsor's due-diligence questionnaire is the gate between a data room and a prospectus. Today it is answered by hand — line by line, document by document — under the pressure of a fixed audit window.

800–1,400line items per questionnaire

Each requires the right document, the right clause, and an answer a sponsor can sign.

2–4months of a CFO's time

Senior finance and company-secretarial teams diverted from the business at the worst possible moment.

A missedaudit window

When the comfort period lapses, the cost is measured in millions — and a delayed listing.

03  The platform

Four agents. One verifiable answer.

Every answer passes through a disciplined pipeline before a human ever sees it. Nothing is asserted without a source; nothing ships without review.

Stage 01
Refiner

Categorises the virtual data room and maps every document to the questionnaire structure.

Stage 02
Drafting Agent

Producing a draft answer for each DDQ item with a direct hyperlink to the source document and page number.

Stage 03
Critic Agent

Challenges each draft against its sources, scores confidence, and flags anything unsupported.

Stage 04
Human Verification

Low-confidence items are gated for the sponsor or CFO to confirm before sign-off.

Section 4 — Financial & Accounting
Describe the issuer's revenue recognition policy and confirm consistency across the track-record period.

The Group recognises revenue when control of goods or services transfers to the customer, in accordance with HKFRS 15 [DR-1042 §4.2]. For long-term service contracts, revenue is recognised over time using an input method [DR-1042 §4.5].

The policy has been applied consistently across the FY2022–FY2024 track-record period, and the auditor identified no restatements or material adjustments [AUD-07 p.31].

04  Trust & security

Built to the standard of a live filing.

The questionnaire is privileged, price-sensitive, and regulated. Our controls are designed for the firms whose licence depends on getting it right.

S/01

Zero-retention private cloud

Your data room is processed in an isolated, single-tenant environment. No content is retained for model training, and nothing leaves your jurisdiction's boundary.

S/02

A citation on every answer

Every claim resolves to a specific document and clause in the data room. No source, no answer — traceability is enforced by the pipeline, not added afterwards.

S/03

Human-in-the-loop verification

The sponsor remains the author of record. Cogent Base drafts and evidences; your team reviews, edits, and signs. Accountability never leaves the firm.

S/04

Confidence-gated output

Each answer carries a calibrated confidence score. Anything below threshold is withheld from the questionnaire and routed for review — never silently asserted.

Compliance Aligned to the regulatory rigour expected of a HKEX filing.
